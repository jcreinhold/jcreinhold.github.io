I’m an electrical engineering Ph.D. student at the Johns Hopkins University working in the Image Analysis and Communication Lab. My research focuses on medical image analysis, and I am currently developing deep learning-based image segmentation methods for structural brain images to help understand multiple sclerosis.

I am a firm believer in creating reproducible research, which includes the creation of very usable and well-documented tools. So I try, when possible, to release code from projects that I have worked on or I am currently working on. Check out my github for a small collection of projects.
