Image entropy

Procedure: For simplicity, we convert the uploaded image to grayscale, and then compute the histogram of the intensities.

The histogram, normalized to sum to one, will represent the probability distribution over the 256 possible image values. (There are 256 possible values because the images are uploaded as an array of unsigned 8-bit integers.)

We use this probability distribution to compute the (base-2) entropy of the image, which is shown next to the "entropy" field. Note that the maximum entropy possible is 8.0, which corresponds to the uniform distribution over the 256 values.
